Welcome to Duluth!

Why I Chose Duluth

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because it’s close to home. Having grown up in Duluth, I’ve always appreciated the city’s welcoming community and natural beauty, which make UMD the perfect environment to continue my education. The campus offers a great balance between academic focus and outdoor inspiration. UMD also provides an affordable, high-quality education.

What I Love About Duluth

The city’s location along Lake Superior makes every season feel special—whether it’s hiking the trails in the summer, watching the leaves change in the fall, or enjoying the peaceful winter views. I’ve always appreciated how connected Duluth is to the outdoors, and how the community feels close-knit and welcoming.
